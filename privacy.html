Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

The short version: we collect only what we need to tell you when PAXO launches. We do not sell your information, we do not share it with advertisers, and you can ask us to delete it at any time by emailing privacy@paxoride.com.

Who we are

This policy covers paxoride.com, operated by Paxo Technologies LLC ("PAXO", "we", "us"), a Pennsylvania limited liability company. PAXO is building a zero-commission rideshare platform launching in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, pending approval from the Pennsylvania Public Utility Commission.

What this policy covers

This policy applies only to information you give us through the waitlist forms on this website. It does not cover the PAXO mobile apps, which are not yet available. When those launch, we will publish a separate, fuller privacy policy covering ride data, location, and payments.

What we collect

We collect only what you type into the waitlist form. Nothing more.

If you join asWe collect
A driver Name, phone number, email, whether you hold a valid Pennsylvania driver's licence (yes/no), whether you have vehicle insurance (yes/no), preferred driving area, and availability.
A rider Name, email, phone number, ZIP code or neighbourhood, and a referral code if you enter one.
We deliberately do not collect driver's licence numbers, insurance policy details, vehicle identification, bank details, or government IDs at this stage. Those are only requested during real driver onboarding inside the app, after launch and after regulatory approval.

Our hosting provider also records standard technical information automatically, such as your IP address, browser type, and the time of your visit. This is used for security and to keep the site running, not to build a profile of you.

Why we collect it

  • To contact you about the launch — the main reason. We will email or text you when PAXO opens in your area.
  • To understand demand — how many drivers and riders are interested, and in which neighbourhoods, so we know where to launch first.
  • To give you what we promised — founding driver priority, or founding rider credit.
  • To prevent spam and abuse — filtering out automated or fraudulent submissions.

We will not use your information for any purpose unrelated to the PAXO launch without asking you first.

Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with advertisers or data brokers.

We use a small number of service providers to run this website, who process data on our behalf:

  • Netlify — hosts this website and stores waitlist form submissions.
  • Namecheap — provides our domain name.

We may also disclose information if we are legally required to — for example, in response to a valid court order, or a lawful request from the Pennsylvania Public Utility Commission or another regulator.

How long we keep it

We keep waitlist information until the earlier of: PAXO launching and you becoming a user (at which point your account information is governed by our full app privacy policy); you asking us to delete it; or 24 months from the date you signed up. If PAXO does not launch, we will delete all waitlist data within 90 days of that decision.

Your choices and rights

  • Ask what we hold — request a copy of the information we have about you.
  • Correct it — tell us if anything is wrong and we will fix it.
  • Delete it — ask us to remove you from the waitlist entirely.
  • Stop hearing from us — unsubscribe at any time; every email will include a link.

Email privacy@paxoride.com and we will respond within 30 days. You do not need to give a reason.

Cookies

This website does not use advertising or tracking cookies. Our hosting provider may set essential cookies required for the site to function and to protect against spam.

How we protect your information

The site is served over HTTPS, and form submissions are transmitted encrypted. Access to waitlist data is limited to people at PAXO who need it. No system is perfectly secure, which is one of the reasons we ask for as little as possible at this stage.

Children

PAXO is not intended for anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, email us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. If the change is significant — for example, if we start using your information for a new purpose — we will email everyone on the waitlist before it takes effect.
